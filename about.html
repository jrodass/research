Who I am

Associate Professor at Universidad Estatal de Milagro (UNEMI), Ecuador. Ph.D. in Computer Engineering awarded Cum Laude with International Distinction by Universidad de Sevilla, Spain.

My academic and professional work connects business intelligence, digital transformation, software engineering and educational innovation. I am particularly interested in turning data, systems and institutional knowledge into better academic services and decisions.

Research interests

Business intelligence, data warehouses, software product lines, recommender systems, digital transformation and educational data mining.

Institutional contribution

I work on the modernization of academic processes, technology-supported education and the design of scalable institutional solutions.
