Research, data and higher education.

Associate Professor at Universidad Estatal de Milagro (UNEMI), Ecuador. Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from Universidad de Sevilla, Spain, awarded Cum Laude with International Distinction. Director of Academic Process Innovation, with over 15 years of experience in university teaching, research, and academic leadership. His work focuses on Business Intelligence, Digital Transformation, Educational Data Mining, and Software Engineering.
